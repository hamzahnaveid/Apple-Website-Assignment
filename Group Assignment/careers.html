Apple's Culture & Shared Values

At Apple they claim that their values inspire the work they share with everyone. Those values are also why they're committed to leaving the world better than what they found it. Each of them contributes to that effort in our own way, bringing a passion for what they do best and what they believe matters most.

Apple also believes that by having a laid back work culture that then their workers are more motivated and have a better work ethic, and that having diversity within the workplace is vital.

Benefits

  2. Discounted stock purchase options.

  4. Access to medical professionals anywhere, virtual and in person.
  5. Gym credit.
  6. Fertility treatments.
