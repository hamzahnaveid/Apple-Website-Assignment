Overview

Introduction:

Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company based in Cupertino, California, USA. It was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. Apple is known for its innovative products and designs, and it has become one of the largest technology companies in the world. Apple also offers a range of software and services, including the iOS and macOS operating systems, the App Store, iCloud storage, and Apple Music streaming service.

Steve Jobs Steve Wozniak Ronald Wayne

Featured Apple Products:

  • iPhone: a smartphone that has become one of the most popular devices in the world.
  • iPad: a tablet computer that is used for a wide range of tasks, including browsing the internet, watching videos, and playing games.
  • Mac: a line of computers that are popular among creative professionals and everyday users alike.
  • Apple Watch: a smartwatch that allows users to stay connected, track fitness goals, and more.
  • AirPods: wireless earbuds that have become increasingly popular in recent years.
