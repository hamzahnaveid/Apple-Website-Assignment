Apple Services

At Apple a wide range of innovative and exciting services are available to the customers. The main five notable services are: Apple Pay, iCloud, Apple Music and they also offer services such as Apple TV+ and Apple Fitness+. At Apple, their aim is to stay connected with their customers and this is done by making sure their services are up-to-date and relevant. In order to purchase these services apple offers different payment plans that suit every individuals needs with a choice of three different payment plans!

Apple Pay

With just one tap of your phone or Apple watch, you can now pay within seconds with no need to carry around cash or cards! Apple took into account the convenience of contactless payments when deciding on new services to bring forth to its customers.

With special care taken into building in privacy and security, Apple promises its customers that their personal data is protected while making transactions by providing them with a specila unique code at every transaction and by never storing your card number on your device or any of the Apple servers nor are they shared with of the merchants.

Apple Pay is supported by the majority of the major banks worldwide, making transactions abroad hassle free. Apple Pay isn't only a contactless payment method as it also works with online purchasing by simplifying the payment process as your information is already securely stored.

iCloud Services

iCloud is a cloud service that was developed by Apple Inc. This cloud service is used to store personal data such as photos, files, notes and mail. Not only are you storing your data on here but it is fully protected by up-to-date security and safety protection. The user can then access all of their data from any synced Apple devices such as iPad or MacBooks, other small features provided by iCloud are contact synchronisation and calendar services.

A very innovative feature iCloud offers is 'Find My iPhone', which allows the users to locate their lost or even stolen Apple devices. iCloud also offers a platform for developers to create cloud-based applicatiobns that can then be accessed by Apple users.

Apple Music

Apple Music is a streaming service offered by Apple, that allows its users to stream millions of songs from all types of music artists. Apple Music also recommends songs and artists to the user based on their listening habits and builds an algorithm upon that.

Apple Music also offers a social feature called "Connect", which allows the artists to interact with their fan base by sharing exclusive content. Additionally Apple Music also offers a 24/7 global radio station called "Beats 1" and it features celebrity DJs and exclusive interviews.

Apple Music is available across all Apple devices such as iPhone, Macs and iPads, however it can also be accessed on non-Apple devices such as Android smartphones and Windows PC. Apple offers four different payment plans to he users, all of which adhere to different individuals.

Apple TV

Apple TV+ is a digital media player and streaming service developed by Apple. It allows its user to stream millions of TV shows and movies, as well as music and photos. There are various features integrated within it such as Siri integration, which allows users to search for content using just their voice.

There is also AirPlay, which allows the user to stream content from their Apple devices onto their TV. Additionally, Apple Tv supports various video and audio formats, including 4K HDR, Dolby Vision, and Dolby Atmos, providing a high-quality viewing and listening experience.

In addition to the hardware device, Apple also offers a streaming service called Apple TV+, which provides exclusive original content, such as TV shows and movies, produced by Apple. Apple TV+ is available to subscribers for a monthly fee and can be accessed on various Apple devices and smart TVs.

Apple Fitness+

Apple Fitness+ is a subscription-based fitness service offered by Apple INC. It is designed to help users stay active and healthy by providing them with guided workouts through their Apple devices. This service offers a variety of workout styles, including strength, yoga, cycling, treadmill and much more, all led by expert trainers.

Apple Fitness+ integrates with the Apple watch, allowing users to track their fitness metrics such as heart rate, calories burned and activity minutes in real time during their workouts. Apple also offers its Apple Fitness+ users the option of competing with friends whom also use Fitness+ using the SharePlay feature.

This service also offers personalised workout recommendations based on the user's fitness level, workout history and interests. Included in this service is a "Workout of the Week" and "MindfullCooldown" sessions to help users stay motivated and relaxed.

Apple Fitness+ is available for a monthly fee and can be accessed through the Fitness app on Apple devices such as iPhone, iPad or even Apple TV.
